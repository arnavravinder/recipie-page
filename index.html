Appetizers

Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and brush with olive oil.
  3. Toast in the oven for 5-7 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, basil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  5. Top each baguette slice with the tomato mixture and serve.

Main Courses

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Marinate chicken breasts in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  5. Serve with your favorite side dishes.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add water, oil, vanilla, and vinegar. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased 9-inch round baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow to cool before serving.

Beverages

Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5 cups water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup of water. Heat until sugar is dissolved to make a syrup.
  2. In a pitcher, combine lemon juice, syrup, and remaining water. Stir well.
  3. Serve over ice and garnish with lemon slices.
